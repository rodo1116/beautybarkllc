Service Duration Disclaimer

The duration times for grooming and baths are approximate and can vary based on factors like how busy we are, whether your dog is a new client, and your dog’s behavior during the session. If you need your dog done by a specific time or prefer a quicker turnaround, we offer an Express Fee to guarantee faster service.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much will my pet’s haircut cost?

Our grooming services are based on weight, coat condition, and requested services. Base prices by weight are listed and subject to change. (Pets are subject to weigh‑in at time of appointment.)

Will my dog get relief breaks during their haircut?

Yes! We give your dog a relief break every 2–3 hours during their spa day.

What vaccinations are required at The Beauty Bark?

Dogs: Rabies, Distemper Parvo, and Bordetella.
Cats: Rabies and FVRCP.
