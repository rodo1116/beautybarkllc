About The Beauty Bark

Opening The Beauty Barks marks a culmination of many years of sacrifice, dedication to our craft, and pride in our work. Our passion for animals and over 15 years of experience has spurred us to improve the way we provide service for pets and their owners. We’re a boutique grooming studio focused on gentle handling, clean ingredients, and a calm spa experience for dogs and cats. Every appointment is tailored to your pet’s temperament and coat needs.

Our team combines precision grooming with a warm, low‑stress environment. From first‑time puppies to seniors and special‑care pets, we meet them where they are.

Gentle, Low‑Stress Care

One‑on‑one attention and fear‑free handling help your pet feel safe from check‑in to pickup.

Clean & Safe

Thorough sanitation between appointments and products chosen for sensitive skin.

Spa‑Level Finish

Thoughtful coat styling, tidy trims, and shine‑boosting finish for that fresh‑from‑the‑spa look.

My name is Anna Marie Smith, and I am the proud owner and a certified groomer at The Beauty Bark.
